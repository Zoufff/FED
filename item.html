Product details

Defined by an oversized fit and large patch pocket with strap, this shirt is made of Re-Nylon: a regenerated nylon yarn (ECONYL®) produced from recycled and purified plastic trash collected in the ocean, fishing nets, and textile fiber waste. Prada's historic enameled metal triangle logo decorates the design.
