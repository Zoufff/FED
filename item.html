Product details

Defined by an oversized fit and large patch pocket with strap, this shirt is made of Re-Nylon: a regenerated nylon yarn (ECONYL®) produced from recycled and purified plastic trash collected in the ocean, fishing nets, and textile fiber waste. Prada's historic enameled metal triangle logo decorates the design.

SEA BEYOND

SEA BEYOND is the educational program launched in 2019 by Prada Group and UNESCO’s Intergovernmental Oceanographic Commission (IOC). to raise awareness on sustainability and ocean preservation.

As of July 2023, the SEA BEYOND project is supported by 1% of the proceeds from the Prada Re-Nylon Collection, to continuously strengthen the partnership between the Prada Group and IOC/UNESCO.
